Sourdough Biscuits

Yield: 5 large biscuits

Ingredients Quantity
All Purpose flour 1 cup
baking powder 2 tsp
salt 3/4 tsp
unsalted butter, cold 8 tbsp
sourdough starter, unfed 1 cup

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 425Â°F, with a rack in the upper third. Line a baking sheet with parchment.

Step 2: Combine the flour, baking powder, and salt. Work the butter into the flour until the mixture is unevenly crumbly.

Step 3: Add the starter, mixing gently until the dough is cohesive.

Step 4: Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface, and gently pat it into a round about 1"-thick.

Step 5: Using a biscuit cutter cut two rounds, or as many as possible. Reshape the dough and repeat until there's none left.

Step 6: Place biscuits onto the baking sheet. Bake for around 25 minutes, until golden brown.
