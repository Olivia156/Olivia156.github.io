Carrot Cake

Yield: 14 servings

Ingredients Quantity
Sugar 2 cups
Flour 2 cups
Salt 1 tsp
Cinnamon 2 tsp
Baking soda 1 tsp
Baking powder 1 tsp
Vegetable oil 1 cup
Eggs 4
Grated carrots 3 cups (1 lb)

Instructions

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease two 9" round pans.

Step 2: Combine the sugar, flour, salt, cinnamon, soda, oil.

Step 3: With an electric mixer beat in the eggs one at a time. Stir in the carrots and the walnuts.

Step 4: Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for about 40 minutes.
