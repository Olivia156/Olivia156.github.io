Braised Lamb

Yield: 4 to 6 servings

Ingredients Quantity
lamb shoulder 2 lbs
pitted prunes 1 cup
minced garlic 1 tbsp
chopped onion 1
minced ginger 2 tsp
cinnamon 1/4 tsp
red wine 1 1/2 cup
stock or water 1/2 cup

Instructions

Step 1: Cut lamb into 2-inch cubes. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, and brown in a large skillet over medium-high heat; remove.

Step 2: Add onion, garlic, prunes, ginger, cinnamon, salt and pepper; cook until fragrant. Add wine, stock or water and browned lamb. When the liquid boils, lower heat to a simmer, cover and cook until tender, 1 1/2 to 2 1/2 hours. Garnish: Parsley.
