Cold Kimchi Noodles

Yield: 2 Servings

Ingredients Quantity
noodles 6.3 oz
kimchi 1/2 cup, chopped
eggs 2, soft boiled
gochujang 2 tbsp
rice vinegar 1 tbsp
honey 1 tbsp
raw sugar 1 tbsp
sesame oil 1 tbsp

Instructions

Step 1: Mix gochujang, rice vinegar, honey, sugar, and sesame oil in a bowl until well combined.

Step 2: Cook noodles according to package directions. Drain under cold water.

Step 3: Mix kimchi, sauce, and noodles in a large bowl until noodles are evenly coated.

Step 4: Serve with egg on top, halved.

Source: My Korean Kitchen
