Chocolate Overnight Oats

Yield: 1 serving

Ingredients Quantity
rolled oats 1/2 cup
cocoa powder 1 1/2 tbsp
chia seeds 2 tsp
salt 1 pinch
plain yogurt 1/2 cup
peanut butter 1 1/2 tbsp
milk 1/2 cup
maple syrup 3 tsp

Instructions

Step 1: Combine oats, cocoa powder, chia, and salt. Add the other ingredients and stir until evenly combined. Refrigerate overnight.

Source: Chocolate Covered Katie
